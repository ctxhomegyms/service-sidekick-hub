Privacy Policy — CTX Home Gyms

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 23, 2026

Fix A Gym Field is a field service management tool operated by CTX Home Gyms ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you interact with our services, including SMS messaging.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide:

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Service address and location
  • Communication preferences and SMS consent status
  • Service history and appointment details

Automatically Collected Information:

  • Device information, browser type, and operating system
  • IP address
  • Usage data and interaction with our platform

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: To schedule appointments, dispatch technicians, and deliver field services
  • SMS Communications: To send appointment reminders, technician en-route alerts, job completion updates, and scheduling notifications (only with your consent)
  • Email Communications: To send service confirmations and updates
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our platform
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

3. SMS Communications & Your Phone Number

If you opt in to receive SMS messages, we will use your mobile phone number solely to send service-related text messages. These include appointment reminders, technician arrival notifications, job status updates, and scheduling confirmations.

We will not sell, rent, share, or disclose your phone number or any personal information to third parties for marketing or promotional purposes.

Your phone number is shared only with our SMS delivery provider (Twilio) for the sole purpose of delivering your service-related messages. Twilio processes your data in accordance with their own privacy policy and is contractually prohibited from using your data for any other purpose.

You can opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. For help, reply HELP. For full details, see our SMS Terms & Conditions.

4. Information Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With technology providers who assist in operating our platform (e.g., SMS delivery via Twilio, cloud hosting). These providers are contractually bound to use your data solely for delivering our services.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, subpoena, or court order
  • Safety: To protect the rights, property, or safety of CTX Home Gyms, our customers, or the public
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with notice to affected users

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including encryption of data in transit and at rest, secure authentication, role-based access controls, and regular security assessments.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, or as required by law. When your information is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it.

7. Your Rights

You may have the right to:

  • Access a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of SMS communications by replying STOP

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@ctxhomegyms.com.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
