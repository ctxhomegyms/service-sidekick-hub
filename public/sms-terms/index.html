SMS Messaging Program Terms

By providing your mobile phone number and opting in to receive SMS messages from our service, you agree to the following terms and conditions.

Program Description

Our service provides field service management that enables service providers to communicate with their customers via SMS text messages. Messages are sent to keep customers informed about scheduled services, appointments, and job-related updates.

Types of Messages You May Receive

  • Appointment reminders (e.g., 24 hours and/or 1 hour before an appointment)
  • Service confirmations and scheduling updates
  • Technician en route notifications
  • Job completion updates
  • Missed-call follow-up notifications

Consent & Opt-In

By providing your mobile phone number to your service provider, you consent to receive automated SMS text messages related to your service appointments and job-related communications.

Consent is not required as a condition of purchasing any goods or services.

How to Opt-Out (Stop Messages)

Reply STOP to any message to opt out. You will receive a one-time confirmation that you have been unsubscribed and no further messages will be sent.

To re-subscribe, reply START or contact your service provider.

Other opt-out keywords accepted: STOPALL, UNSUBSCRIBE, CANCEL, END, QUIT.
