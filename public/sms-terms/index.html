SMS Messaging Program Terms

By providing your mobile phone number and opting in to receive SMS messages from FixAGym Field, you agree to the following terms and conditions.

Program Description

FixAGym Field provides field service management software that enables service providers to communicate with their customers via SMS text messages. Messages are sent to keep customers informed about scheduled services, appointments, and job-related updates.

Types of Messages You May Receive

  • Appointment reminders (e.g., 24 hours and/or 1 hour before an appointment)
  • Service confirmations and scheduling updates
  • Technician en route notifications
  • Job completion updates

Consent & Opt-In

By providing your mobile phone number to your service provider who uses FixAGym Field, you consent to receive automated SMS text messages related to your service appointments and job-related communications.

Consent is not required as a condition of purchasing any goods or services.

How to Opt-Out (Stop Messages)

Reply STOP to any message to opt out. You will receive a one-time confirmation message that you have been unsubscribed.

To re-subscribe, reply START or contact your service provider.

Need Help?

Reply HELP to any message for assistance.

Message Frequency & Rates

Message frequency varies based on your service activity and appointment schedule. Message and data rates may apply.

Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

Privacy

Your phone number and related personal information will be used only to provide the SMS communications described above and will not be sold or shared for marketing.
